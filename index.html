The Grades

Join him on the adventure of a lifetime in THE GRADES.

JailBreak

Home. That was the only thing on multi-billionaire toy company CEO, Oliver Ullyses’s (Stephen Amell) mind after being falsely accused and arrested for the murder of his long-time rival. After three long years in jail he breaks out and embarks on a perilous journey to return to the life he once had. But he can’t do it alone. With the help of family friend Eugene Maes (Dane DeHaan) and his former advisor Sully Choure (Tim DeKay) with aid of a mysterious new ally he makes the trip home. However, someone is after him, someone powerful, someone who wants him dead, someone who will stop at nothing.

The One The Only

After refusing to break up with his poor girlfriend Selena (Selena Cheung), valiant Justin Lartes (Foram Patel) is forced to enter the 56th Selection Games- a suicide competition where 8 brave men compete to win Princess Clara’s (Keith Yeung) hand in marriage. Despite the appealing advantages that come with marrying the princess, Justin is not competing for her, but for his freedom. He must go through a series of grueling obstacles including fighting to the death to prove his etiquette, intelligence, leadership and strength. Losing the games is not an option for Justin as it results in execution. All he wishes for is the chance to run away with Selena. Will Justin survive the gruesome games and return to his true love?

Trapped Inside

Odyssea, played by Xandria Crosland, races against time to get to her basketball game. As she leaves Townsend Harris High School, she has to go through numerous nerve-racking obstacles posed by her teachers, Mr. Dundun and Mr. Bardun. Escaping from her nightmare every flight of stairs she goes, trouble awaits for her. There is a never ending cycle of fear and unpredictable events ahead of her. Each step that Odyssea takes towards the doors of freedom, the feeling of trepidation rushes into her. Why are her teachers after this girl? Will Odyssea ever get to her game on time and escape from hell? Odyssea thought school was tough. Now she has learned that leaving it is tougher.

Trapped Inside

HE’S NOT THE FIRST, BUT HE MAY BE THE LAST. It’s the year 2075, where 0.17% of modernized America’s population is clinically disabled. Enter a dystopian world where being handicapped is frowned upon and experimentation for the “greater good” is standard. Zoom in a little further into the life of a crippled, and loved, husband and father. #10 wakes up to find himself captured and in the process of being tested on. Follow his journey as he tries to get back home. Throw in an evil scientist, a secretly kind accomplice, and a son who is ready to dig up some dirt, and things get a lot more complicated. He's not the first, but he may be the last.
