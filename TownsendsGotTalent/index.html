The Perfect Four

The Perfect Four were a group that dedicated their time towards performing in Harrisfest, the school-wide talent show. They met up every saturday, sunday, and off days during robotics to prepare their performance, but due to complications they never made it onto the stage. However, now they come back together to perform their cover of "Perfect" by Ed Sheeran, a famous English songwriter and guitarist.

Guitarists: Amar M (fingerstyle) & Andy H (chords)
Ukuleleists: Neeharika K (chords) & Savannah S (chords)
Singers: Neeharika K, Savannah S, & Andy H

The students here at Townsend Harris High School pursue many interesting hobbies that have slowly evolved into a talent that only few have.
