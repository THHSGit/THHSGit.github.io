Meet The Board
Manish Tripathi
President
Manish Tripathi works at an investment bank as an Executive Director. He is global head of Quality Assurance for algorithmic trading and manages a wide variety of regulatory projects across Asia, Europe and North America. Manish lives in Mobile, AL. He loves good food, and enjoys traveling. In his spare time, he enjoys cooking, and has recently started learning some basic carpentry skills.
Annabelle Fonseca
Secretary
Annabelle Fonseca is a Surgical oncologist specializing in complex gastrointestinal cancers. She lives in Mobile, AL and works at the University of South Alabama where she treats patients with liver, pancreas and foregut cancer. She loves to read (history, philosophy and other non-fiction), bake and travel.
Prashant Murti
Treasurer
Prashant Murti is a commodities trader at a major investment bank. He has two decades of experience in the design, operation, and trading of electricity and natural gas markets in North America. Prashant lives in Houston with his wife and two children. He enjoys cycling, swimming, and walking his two rescue mutts.

Michael Sullivan
Vice President
Michael Sullivan is an Endocrine Surgeon who treats patients at the Hackensack Meridien Health in New Jersey. He loves outdoor adventures, reading and wine.
Katherine Wetmur
Board Member
Katherine Wetmur is a Managing Director and Head of Technology & Operational Risk at a major investment bank. Prior to this, she was the International CIO and managed the firm’s diverse global technology footprint. Katherine holds a B.S in Materials Science from Rice University. A long-standing champion of diversity, Katherine is a member of Morgan Stanley’s EMEA Diversity Action Council, is an Executive Sponsor of the Pride and Ally Network and a member of the Mental Health Advisory Board. She is a strong supporter of the Firm’s initiatives to foster STEM education and encourage young women to pursue technical career.
Carole Wiseman Norden
Board Member
Carole Wiseman Norden is a Medical Oncologist at a rural hospital in southern Alabama that provides care to an underserved population of the state. She has a deep commitment to providing state of the art cancer care in a thoughtful and compassionate manner to people who have financial or health challenges which make it difficult for them to travel as well as to those in the area who would just prefer to remain close to home to receive their cancer care. She enjoys traveling with her family, spending a week or two each year in her home away from home, New York City, seeing plays, enjoying dance and music performances and just wandering the streets and parks.
